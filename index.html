Get to know me!

Jason Liu

Student

Hi! I'm Jason.


Only read this if you want to know too much about me. Honestly, I'm not sure what to say. I'm from the great state of Virginia, near DC. However, I currently attend University of Wisconsin-Madison, planning to major in CS.

I love to workout, watch movies, and read. My favorite movie is "Pulp Fiction." It completely blew my mind. My favorite book is "A Short History of Nearly Everything" by Bill Bryson. I love food, especially sushi. Seriously, I want to eat sushi literally 24/7.

If you've made it this far, you're really committed. So, I'll tell you the final important things. Firstly, tabs not spaces. I cannot emphasize this enough. Secondly, vim is better than emac. Don't let anyone else tell you differently. These two facts are fundamentally true. Lastly, sublime is the best editor.
